Locally Sourced

Designing, creating, and building guitars since 2014. Every piece of wood is locally sourced in San Diego. All truly handcrafted, cut, and hand sanded. All pickguards hand cut from different materials. If you're looking for a breathe of fresh air or something your friend doesn't have in their collection, look no further. No one guitar is the same!

About me

ABCO is a guitar company started by Aaron Bowen, out of his house in southern California. A classically trained guitarist who has 40+ years of expierence. He thinks outside of the box and brings a unique look to a world where most guitars look the same. You'll never be disappointed with his work wheather it be guitars or music. When he's not building guitars he spends time with his wife Kate and their adorable cat Edie.
